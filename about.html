Oi! My name is Izabella Timmons.
I’m a User Experience Designer, born and raised in Brazil, now living in Austin, TX.

For the first seven years of my career, I worked as an electrician in a steel mill. During this time my primary responsibilities included maintenance and implementation of large overhead cranes used to move vats of molten steel. I enjoyed the challenge and new technologies and proving my value in a gritty masculine environment.

Next, I transitioned to a role in customer service where I was responsible for ensuring positive customer experiences with a globally recognized luxury brand assisting their clients to fully understand products - as well as responding to customers who had not had a great product experience.

Both experiences helped me gain skills in communication, teamwork, problem-solving, quick learning, time management, and process improvement. I've learned that to solve a problem it is crucial to understand the problem statement and underlying consumer need.

While these previous roles were challenging my passion since childhood has been art and design which is why I have decided to make a career change to align my career with doing something I love.

Bloc.io - an online Design bootcamp apprenticeship has given me the opportunity to complete my UX studies and gain practical experience working on real-world projects. I am passionate about solving problems through design with simple and beautiful solutions.

When I’m not thinking about (or actually doing) design, I love traveling, spending time with friends, going to concerts or for a walk in the park.
